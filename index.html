HOW TO USE
👁 WRAITH — SPIRIT VOICE
Organic frequency-based spirit communication. The theory: spirits manipulate electromagnetic frequencies to select and transmit fragments of sound. What comes through is chosen by the environment — not by the app.
How to use
1. Tap ACTIVATE WRAITH
2. Allow microphone access
3. Remain still and silent
4. Wait for fragments to come through
5. Listen carefully to what breaks the silence
Tip
Ask a question out loud, then wait in silence. The spirit may respond through the next fragment. Longer pauses yield better results.
Sensitivity — how much energy triggers a response
Clip Length — duration of each fragment
Reverb — adds ethereal depth to the voice
Distort — raw, unnatural quality to fragments
📻 SWEEP — SPIRIT BOX
Sweeps continuously through live FM and AM radio frequencies. Real radio signals — no pre-recorded audio. Silence between stations creates space for spirit voices to emerge.
How to use
1. Tap INITIATE SCAN
2. Select FM or AM band
3. Adjust Speed (slower = more time on each station)
4. Listen for words that stand out from static
5. Use REVERSE DIRECTION to sweep backward
Ghost Hz — 18.98Hz infrasound tone, theorized to induce feelings of presence
Echo / Reverb — audio effects to help isolate voices
Volume — output level of the sweep
Tip
Words that appear consistently across multiple sweeps are stronger evidence. Record sessions and review afterward.
🎙 EVP — ELECTRONIC VOICE
Records audio through your microphone with optional filtering. EVP theory holds that spirits can imprint voices onto recordings that weren't audible during capture.
How to use
1. Select your audio filter
2. Tap RECORD EVP
3. Ask questions — leave silence between each
4. Tap STOP RECORDING when done
5. Play back with headphones for best results
6. Use ✂ trim to isolate interesting sections
RAW — no filtering, records exactly what mic hears
Band-Pass — isolates voice frequency range
Low-Pass — removes hiss and high noise
High-Pass — removes rumble and wind noise
Notch 60Hz — removes electrical hum from outlets
Voice Boost — amplifies speech frequencies
Tip
Many EVP captures are only audible on playback. Review with headphones at higher volume. The TRIM tool lets you isolate specific moments.
